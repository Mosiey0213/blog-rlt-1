Your Red Light Therapy Questions Answered

5 min read

Is red light therapy safe?

Yes, red light therapy is FDA-cleared and has been extensively studied for decades. Unlike UV light, red and near-infrared wavelengths don't damage skin. The therapy is non-invasive, painless, and has no known serious side effects when used as directed.

How long before I see results?

Most people notice improvements in skin texture and radiance within 2-3 weeks of consistent daily use. Reduction in fine lines and wrinkles typically becomes visible around 4-8 weeks. Collagen production continues to improve with ongoing use, so results compound over time.

Can I use it with my current skincare routine?

Absolutely! Red light therapy complements other skincare products beautifully. In fact, it may enhance the absorption of serums and moisturizers. For best results, cleanse your face first, use the red light, then apply your regular products.

Does it work for all skin types?

Yes! Red light therapy is effective and safe for all skin types and tones. The wavelengths used don't rely on melanin absorption like some other treatments, making it universally beneficial without risk of hyperpigmentation or skin damage.

How is this different from tanning beds?

Completely different! Tanning beds use harmful UV light that damages skin and increases cancer risk. Red light therapy uses only red and near-infrared wavelengths that repair and rejuvenate skin without any UV exposure. It's therapeutic, not cosmetic tanning.
